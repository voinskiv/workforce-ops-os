# Product Vision — Workforce Operations Control OS

## Product Idea

The Workforce Operations Control OS is an internal operating system for personnel-intensive operational companies that deploy workers across customers, sites, shifts, accommodation, vehicles, partner companies, and customer-specific rules.

The system does not replace customer systems such as Jager or FIEGE. It creates an internal control layer above them.

## Product North Star

Ensure that the right person is at the right site, in the right shift, with the right status, rules, and validation, so that work becomes visible, billable, controllable, and scalable.

## Strategic Thesis

The company does not primarily need more spreadsheets.

It needs an operational control layer that connects:

Employee → Assignment → Shift → Site → Time → Performance → Billing → Profitability → Management Decision

## First Target Environment

The first implementation is for a company with approximately 180–250 workers operating in personnel services, outsourcing, logistics support, and operational site steering.

The business has:

- multiple customers
- multiple sites
- customer-owned systems
- partner companies
- worker onboarding flows
- shift planning
- time validation
- accommodation constraints
- transport constraints
- manual backoffice processes
- operational exceptions
- management need for weekly profitability visibility

## Long-Term Product Direction

The first MVP is internal. The long-term opportunity is to become a repeatable OS or productized transformation framework for personnel-intensive operational companies such as:

- logistics service providers
- cleaning companies
- production service providers
- workforce outsourcing companies
- multi-site operations companies
- family-owned operational companies that need systematization

## Key Business Question

How can a personnel-intensive operational company become data-driven, less person-dependent, more profitable, and more scalable without replacing all existing customer systems?
