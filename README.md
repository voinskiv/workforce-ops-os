## Product Summary

The system is a control layer for workforce operations in personnel-intensive businesses.

It does not replace customer warehouse systems such as Jager or FIEGE. It helps the company control workforce availability, onboarding, planning, time validation, performance, exceptions, and management visibility across customers, sites, shifts, partner companies, accommodation, and transport constraints.
